What if I'm in crisis right now? +
🆘 Please reach out immediately. Call or text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) — available 24/7, free, and confidential. You can also text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line). You matter, and help is available right now.
Is this website completely free? +
Yes — completely and always free. Every article, tool, journaling prompt and resource on this site is available to anyone without signing up or paying anything. Our mission is access for everyone. Paid products are optional and support our free resources.
Is this a replacement for therapy? +
No — and we're honest about that. Healthy Minds Journal is an educational and supportive resource, not a clinical service. Our content can complement therapy beautifully, but it is not a substitute for professional mental health treatment. If you think you may benefit from therapy, we encourage you to explore our therapist directory.
How do digital downloads work? +
After purchase, you'll receive an immediate download link via email. All files are yours to keep forever. PDFs can be printed or used digitally. Audio files are MP3 format and work on any device. If you lose your files, just email us and we'll resend them — no charge.
What's your refund policy? +
We offer a 30-day happiness guarantee on all digital products. If you're not satisfied, email us for a full refund — no questions asked. For merchandise, we accept returns within 30 days of delivery for store credit or exchange. Donations are non-refundable but 100% tax-deductible where applicable.
How are donations used? +
100% of donations directly support our mission: creating free mental health content, maintaining crisis resource directories, and keeping our platform accessible to everyone. We publish annual transparency reports. Administrative costs are covered by product sales, so your donation goes straight to helping people.
Can I write for Healthy Minds Journal? +
Yes! We welcome submissions from mental health advocates, people with lived experience, and professionals. We believe in the power of diverse voices. Visit our Write for Us page to learn about our guidelines and submit a pitch.
